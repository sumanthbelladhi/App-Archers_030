Honouring our past, embracing our future

Since the very beginning, Casio watch development has been driven by the quest to create all new value in the timepiece. In 1974, Casio introduced the Casiotron, the world’s first digital watch with an automatic calendar function. Now 50 years later, we are celebrating a half-century of innovation since that very first groundbreaking watch. At Casio, we have always challenged ourselves to pursue originality and innovation. We have built a distinctive brand by constantly expanding the potential of the wristwatch. Grateful for 50 years of growth, we are working hard on green technologies and remain committed to building a sustainable world.



This 50th anniversary offers a chance to look back on the history of Casio watches, as well as to forge new connections as we boldly embrace the challenges that will take us into the future. We are proud to present a lineup of special commemorative models designed to symbolise our determination to stay true to who we are while constantly innovating. Their use of meticulous design expresses the omnipresent yet ever-changing nature of the sky, the sea, and the sunlight that makes them shine today and forevermore. You can count on Casio to keep designing amazing watches the world has never seen before, always guided by our determination to create new value.
